Frequently Asked Questions

What is an online exam?

Please prepare a computer attached to the camera and take the exam.

Please tell me about student meals. Do you have halal foods?

Student meals are basically white rice + 2 to 3 side dishes. Khmer, Indian, and Western dishes are available, and also we can serve Halal foods as well.

Can I do shopping?

There is a shop on the premises where you can purchase daily necessities.Also, on weekends, we have a shuttle bus to and from the university and Phnom Penh, so students can go shopping to the AEON MALL in the city center or a nearby market.
